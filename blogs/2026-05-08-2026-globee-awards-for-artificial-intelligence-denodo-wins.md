2026 Globee® Awards for Artificial Intelligence: Denodo Wins Silver for Emerging AI Technology Denodo Technologies, Inc. has been named a Silver Globee® Winner in the prestigious Globee® Awards for Artificial Intelligence. This distinction celebrates the Denodo Platform as a trusted data foundation for agentic and generative AI, earning the title of Emerging AI Technology of the Year. The award honors Denodo’s remarkable accomplishments and sets a benchmark of excellence for peers across industries, serving as a testament to the team's hard work, vision, and lasting impact in the field.
