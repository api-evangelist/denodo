IDC Data and AI Summit Denodo is a proud sponsor of the Data and AI Summit themed "Unlocking the Power of Data and AI". The IDC Data and AI Summit is the premier event for senior technology and business executives driving artificial intelligence and data strategies across the region. Designed for CDOs, Heads of Data, and AI and innovation leaders, this full-day event delivers high-impact insights and actionable strategies focused on organizations’ top priorities: scaling AI adoption, ensuring data quality and governance, and navigating complex digital regulation and readiness.
