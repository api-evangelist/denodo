<span>The AI Trust Gap Briefing</span>

            <div class="field field--name-field-description field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field__item"><p dir="ltr"><span>AI is no longer answering questions — it is making decisions and taking actions in your day-to-day operations. This shift raises the bar from&nbsp;</span><em><span>“Is it useful?”</span></em><span> to a far more critical question:&nbsp;</span><em><span>“Can we trust it?”</span></em></p><p dir="ltr"><span>Based on research with 850 enterprise leaders across 16 countries and 6 sectors, this session explains why AI succeeds in pilots but fails at scale. We’ll cover the gaps behind this pattern and the three foundations required to operationalize agentic AI: live data, the right data, and runtime guardrails.</span></p><p dir="ltr"><span>You’ll leave with a clear diagnostic and a practical path to move from experimentation to real business impact.</span></p></div>
