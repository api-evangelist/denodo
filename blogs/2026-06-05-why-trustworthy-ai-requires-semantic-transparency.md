As AI systems become increasingly embedded within enterprise decision-making and operational processes, organizations require more than accurate outputs. They require intelligent systems whose behavior can be understood, inspected, governed, and contextualized within coherent semantic frameworks. Semantic transparency enables enterprises to audit AI decisions, enforce data governance, and maintain trust at scale.
